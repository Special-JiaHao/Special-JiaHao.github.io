    ∵d1∣y,d1∣r\because d_{1}|y, d_{1}|r∵d1​∣y,d1​∣r
    \therefore d_{2}|d_

    ⇒d2∣(qy+r)⇔d2∣x\Rightarrow d_{2}|(qy+r) \Leftrightarrow d_{2}|x⇒d2​∣(qy+r)⇔d2​∣x.
    ∵d2∣x,d2∣y\because d_{2}|x, d_{2}|y∵d2​∣x,d2​∣y
